Chocolate Pizza

posted on 15 Dec 2013/dessserts

For the fig-swirl: Melt butter over medium heat in a saucepan. Add brown sugar and stir to dissolve. Halve all of the figs and toss in the saucepan with water and lemon juice Cook over medium heat stirring frequintly, until you have a chunky-jammy mixture. add sault with one or two stirs, set aside and let cool completely.

Ice cream: In small pot over medium heat, combine milk, and granulated sugar until sugar is completely dissolved and milk is just barely lukewarm. Whisk in the egg yolks. Set mixture in the fridge and wait until the fig mixture is cooled.

Using an ice cream machine, pour liquids into frozen basin and procces according to the manufactor instructions, i.e., let spin and thicken for 20 minutes before adding mascarpone, fig jam mixture, and nuts.Continue to procces for +/- 10 minutes. Pour semi-frozen mixture into a pyrex dish or glass tupperware. Freeze for at least two hours before serving.

  • 1 1/2 cups milk
  • 1/2 cup mascarpone
  • 1/2 tsb pink salt
  • 1lb black Mission Figs
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 2-4 tbsp water
  • 1 1/2 cups heavy cream
  • 1/3 granulated sugar
  • 2 egg yolks
  • 1 lemon, juiced
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 cup honey roasted pecans, roughly chopped
